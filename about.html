Our Mission

To provide a frictionless environment for your personal and business endeavors so that you are able to focus and do what you love instead of getting caught up in the tasks that distract you.

Our Story

Chameleon started as an idea when the three of us got together and described how we each had always wanted to start a business.  That is when we realized that the three of us together would make the perfect team. Together, we could create a business that played to each of our individual strengths and capitalized on our diversity to provide effective solutions for others. Cue, best friends for life.  The only thing left to figure out was what kind of business to operate.  Then it hit us - why not do for other people what we are doing for ourselves? Why not create a business to remove the friction from other businesses. This is our ultimate aim, for now we are sticking to three main categories: social media marketing/management, web development, and branding/design. Later, we hope to offer full services to take companies from 0-100 without any friction on the client's end.

Our Brand is You - we blend in so you stand out.

The three of us came up with this motto when we were talking about how we want to have clients that we believe in. We wanted to stand out as a service provider that prioritized our customers. We provide an atmosphere where our clients can thrive with our support. Thus, our brand is you.

Why Chameleon?

We went through a difficult phase when picking a business name.  Countless possibilities, none of which are available for a domain name.  Finding the balance between trying to sound cool yet have it still pertain to what we actually do. You should have heard some of the things we came up with.  In the end we chose Chameleon because of what we wanted our company to stand for. The principal that we are here to help, but we want to be the unseen, solutions ninja that everyone desires. We want to blend in so you can stand out (and still hopefully get some of the credit).
